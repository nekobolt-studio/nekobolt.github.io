Baking Success

Baking Success is our charming time-management game, where you join Fay Cloud as she renovates a rickety old zeppelin into a flying bakery to save her grandparents' struggling business from ruin. Soaring through whimsical lands with mechanic Reid Wood, she serves delicious pastries to eager customers, unlocks new recipes and upgrades, and spreads joy via hand-drawn cutscenes, fun minigames, and a relaxing soundtrack—across Story, Expert, Challenge, and Endless modes.

Originally crafted in 2013 with C# and the XNA framework, published by Big Fish Games (where it hit the top 10 charts and currently holds a solid 4.3 out of 5 player rating), our special 10th-anniversary Steam edition runs smoothly on low-spec machines for crisp, addictive gameplay!
