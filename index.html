Consulting

With two decades of experience, we're happy to share our knowledge to help you get your projects moving. We offer practical help in several areas:

Game Development

General support with game design, project structure, and technical problem-solving for small to mid-sized projects.

2D & 3D Game Assets

Creation of game-ready 2D and 3D models and textures, optimized and ready to use in your project.

VR Prototyping

Planning and building early-stage VR experiences and interactive prototypes to test your ideas quickly.

Graphic & Web Design

Designing and building websites, along with clean UI/UX and visual materials for your brand or game.

3D Print Design

Designing and modeling your 3D prints, from initial concepts to final ready-to-print models.

DevOps & CI/CD

Setting up automation (like Jenkins) to make building and deploying your project easier and more reliable.
